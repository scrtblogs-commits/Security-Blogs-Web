# SecurityBlogs — Company Overview

## About SecurityBlogs

SecurityBlogs is Australia's only specialist B2B digital marketing agency built exclusively for the physical and cyber security industry. Founded and directed by Yousif Jonaid, the agency serves security installers, integrators, SaaS vendors, manufacturers, and consultants across Australia, the USA, the UK, the UAE, and Singapore.

## Business Model

SecurityBlogs generates revenue across five surfaces:

1. **Core Service Retainers** — Monthly SEO, AIO, AEO, GEO, Google Ads, Bing Ads, and Web Design engagements
2. **Publishing Partnerships** — Guest posting placements, sponsored posts, press release distribution, backlink packages, product promotion, and display advertising through the SecurityBlogs publishing network
3. **Free Tools & Lead Generation** — AI Visibility Score tool, local pack checker, AI Visibility Challenge, and other interactive tools that capture inbound leads
4. **Content Authority** — The Knowledge Hub positions SecurityBlogs as the definitive editorial voice for security marketing, driving organic inbound at scale
5. **Case Studies & Social Proof** — Documented client outcomes (50+ brands, measurable ROI) act as always-on sales collateral
