# Content Cluster Master Plan — SecurityBlogs

## How Topic Clusters Work

A topic cluster consists of:
1. **One pillar page** — a comprehensive overview of a broad topic (2,500–5,000 words)
2. **Multiple supporting pages** — in-depth coverage of specific sub-topics within the cluster (1,000–2,000 words each)
3. **Internal linking structure** — pillar links to all supporting pages; all supporting pages link back to pillar

This structure signals topical authority to Google and creates a logical architecture for both users and AI systems.
