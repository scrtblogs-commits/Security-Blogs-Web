# AI Visibility, AEO & GEO Keywords — SecurityBlogs Keyword Database

## Overview

This database covers keyword targets across three converging disciplines:
- **AEO** (AI-Enabled Optimisation) — featured snippets, AI Overviews, answer boxes
- **GEO** (Geographic Entity Optimisation) — local entities, knowledge panels, local AI results
- **AI Visibility / AIO** (AI Intelligence Optimisation) — LLM citations, AI assistant mentions

These keywords target both SecurityBlogs' own visibility and content strategy.
