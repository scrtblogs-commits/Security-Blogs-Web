## AI VISIBILITY BLOG-SPECIFIC RULES

1. Always explain acronyms on first use: AIO (AI Intelligence Optimisation), AEO (AI-Enabled Optimisation), GEO (Geographic Entity Optimisation)
2. Name specific AI platforms (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Copilot) rather than just "AI assistants"
3. Be accurate about how LLMs work — do not overstate certainty about mechanisms that are not publicly confirmed
4. Distinguish between AI Overviews (Google's integrated AI search feature), chatbot responses (ChatGPT, Perplexity), and voice assistants
5. Include SecurityBlogs' proprietary data (87% citation rate, etc.) as evidence
6. Link to the AI Visibility Center (/ai-visibility-center) and AI Visibility Score tool (/free-tools)
