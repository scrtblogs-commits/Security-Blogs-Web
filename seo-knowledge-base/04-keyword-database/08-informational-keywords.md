## Overview

This keyword database covers informational and educational queries — the top-of-funnel (ToFu) stage of the buyer journey. These are questions and research queries asked by security business owners, marketing managers, and SEO/marketing professionals who are learning about digital marketing disciplines relevant to the physical security industry. Informational content builds topical authority for SecurityBlogs Australia, attracts organic traffic at scale, earns backlinks, and creates entry points into the commercial funnel. These keywords support blog posts, guides, explainers, and resource pages.

4. **how does digital PR help SEO** — Bridges PR and SEO education; builds the case for SecurityBlogs' editorial and sponsored content services. Low difficulty and a topic where SecurityBlogs has genuine authority.
