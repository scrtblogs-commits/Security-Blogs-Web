## Overview

This keyword database covers the Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) space, encompassing both the emerging discipline of optimising for AI-generated search results and the traditional local SEO / "near me" landscape. For SecurityBlogs Australia, GEO represents a dual opportunity: selling GEO services to security companies wanting AI search visibility, and capturing local SEO clients in the physical security trade. Target markets are AU (primary), US, UK, UAE, and SG.

## Quick Wins

Five keywords to target first based on low difficulty, clear intent alignment, and traffic potential:

1. **what is GEO SEO** — Definitional query with growing search volume as GEO awareness increases. A strong explainer article can earn a featured snippet and establish SecurityBlogs as a GEO authority.
2. **how to rank in Google AI Overviews** — High practical intent, growing rapidly as Google AI Overviews become mainstream. Ideal pillar content that drives both organic traffic and GEO service enquiries.
3. **local SEO for security companies Australia** — AU-qualified, strong buyer intent, low competition. Should anchor the local SEO / GEO service page targeting Australian security operators.
4. **how to appear in Google Maps security** — Practical question with local SEO intent; question format earns featured snippets. Maps to GMB optimisation services.
5. **GEO for security companies** — Near-zero competition, directly on-brand. Even a thin, well-structured service page will rank and attract highly qualified buyers.
