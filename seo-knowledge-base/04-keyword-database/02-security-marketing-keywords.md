## Quick Wins

Five keywords to target first based on low difficulty, clear intent alignment, and traffic potential:

1. **how to market a security company** — High informational volume, question format, perfect for a comprehensive guide that captures top-of-funnel traffic and builds authority.
2. **Google Ads for security companies Australia** — AU-qualified, strong buyer intent, directly maps to the Google Ads service offering. Low competition for the geo-specific variant.
3. **security company marketing Australia** — Core service head term with AU qualifier. Low difficulty compared to the global variant; should anchor the main marketing service page.
4. **how to get more security clients** — High volume how-to query; drives leads-focused blog content that naturally funnels into SecurityBlogs services.
5. **marketing for alarm companies** — Vertical-specific, low difficulty, strong commercial intent. A dedicated service or landing page can rank quickly and attract a specific buyer persona.
