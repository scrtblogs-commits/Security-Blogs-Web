## Overview

This keyword database covers the Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO) space — an emerging and fast-growing discipline focused on optimising content to appear in featured snippets, voice search results, AI-generated answers, and structured knowledge panels. SecurityBlogs Australia offers AEO as a distinct, forward-looking service for security industry clients. These keywords are valuable both for attracting security companies that want AEO services and for building SecurityBlogs' own topical authority in the AEO space.

## Quick Wins

Five keywords to target first based on low difficulty, clear intent alignment, and traffic potential:

1. **what is AEO** — Highest-volume definitional term in the AEO space. A well-structured explainer article can capture a featured snippet and drive significant top-of-funnel traffic while establishing SecurityBlogs as an authority.
2. **AEO vs SEO** — High-volume comparison query with strong shareability. Ideal for a pillar blog post that internally links to AEO and SEO service pages.
3. **AEO services Australia** — AU-qualified service term with low competition. Should anchor the AEO service page and drive qualified buyer traffic.
4. **how to get featured snippets** — High volume, question format, actionable content opportunity. Achieves a featured snippet about featured snippets — demonstrating the service in practice.
5. **AEO for security companies** — Near-zero competition, directly on-brand for SecurityBlogs. Even modest traffic converts highly because it is the exact buyer persona searching.
