## Overview

This keyword database covers commercial investigation queries — the middle-of-funnel (MoFu) stage where potential buyers are actively comparing options, reading reviews, and evaluating specific agencies and services before making a purchase decision. These keywords are asked by security business owners, marketing managers, and SEO professionals who are aware of the problem and are now researching specific solutions. Targeting these keywords with comparison content, review pages, case studies, and service comparison guides drives high-quality traffic from buyers close to converting.

## Quick Wins

Five keywords to target first based on commercial intent, low competition, and conversion potential:

1. **best SEO agency security Australia** — Core mid-funnel comparison term. A well-optimised comparison/landing page that positions SecurityBlogs as the specialist choice over generalist agencies will convert high-intent visitors.
2. **AEO vs SEO which is better** — 210 monthly searches, low difficulty, strong commercial investigation intent. An authoritative comparison article earns featured snippets and links while driving AEO service enquiries.
3. **how to choose security SEO agency** — Decision-guide format with high buyer intent. A comprehensive guide that helps security companies evaluate agencies — with SecurityBlogs as the recommended choice — converts strongly.
4. **SecurityBlogs review** — Branded trust query. Ensure a review/testimonials page or About page captures this intent with schema markup for aggregate ratings.
5. **top security marketing companies Australia** — Roundup/comparison format; SecurityBlogs can create this content and position itself within it, earning both the traffic and the authority signals from being referenced in the roundup.
