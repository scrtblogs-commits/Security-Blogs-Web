## Overview

This keyword database covers transactional and bottom-of-funnel (BoFu) keywords — queries made by buyers who are ready to purchase, book, or hire right now. These are the highest-value keywords in the portfolio: lower in volume than informational terms but with conversion rates that can be 10–50x higher. For SecurityBlogs Australia, these keywords should anchor service pages, pricing pages, contact/booking pages, and checkout flows. Targeting these terms precisely with clear calls to action, trust signals, and transparent pricing drives direct revenue.

## Quick Wins

Five keywords to target first based on immediate revenue potential, low competition, and clear conversion opportunity:

1. **security SEO services cost** — 80 monthly searches, low difficulty, and directly maps to a pricing page. Buyers searching this are ready to engage; a transparent pricing page with package options and a booking CTA will convert at high rates.
2. **buy security guest post** — Core transactional term for the guest posting service. A clean service page with package options, sample placements, domain authority stats, and an order form captures this intent directly.
3. **book security SEO consultation** — Low volume but extremely high intent. A booking/contact page with a calendar integration (Calendly or equivalent) targeting this exact phrase can generate consultation bookings with minimal traffic.
4. **Google Ads management security company** — 80 monthly searches, low difficulty. Maps directly to the Google Ads management service; a service page targeting this with case studies and pricing will capture buyers evaluating PPC management.
5. **advertise on SecurityBlogs** — Near-zero competition (branded intent), but critical to capture. An advertise/media kit page targeting this term converts advertisers and sponsors who have already decided to place ads and are looking for rates and process information.
