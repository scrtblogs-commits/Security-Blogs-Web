## Notes for Writers

- AI visibility is the most forward-looking cluster — frame SecurityBlogs Australia as the definitive Australian authority on this emerging discipline
- Australian English throughout (optimise, recognise, licence as noun, etc.)
- When referencing AI tools, always use current names: ChatGPT (OpenAI), Gemini (Google), Perplexity AI, Copilot (Microsoft), Claude (Anthropic)
- Do not overstate certainty — AI citation signals are not fully understood; use phrases like "evidence suggests" and "best practice indicates"
- Include practical prompts readers can use to test their own AI visibility
- Cross-link heavily to Cluster 03 (GEO) and Cluster 07 (PR) — these three clusters form a natural "next-gen visibility" trio
- Each supporting page: 1,500–2,500 words; pillar: 4,000+ words; blog posts: 800–1,500 words
