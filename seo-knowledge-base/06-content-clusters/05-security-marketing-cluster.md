## Notes for Writers

- Australian English throughout (optimise, colour, licence as noun, practise as verb, etc.)
- Reference Australian privacy law context where relevant (Privacy Act 1988, Australian Spam Act 2003 for email marketing)
- Security industry has unique advertising restrictions on Google and Meta — document these clearly in relevant posts
- Include pricing benchmarks where possible: typical CPCs for security keywords in Australia, average email open rates for security industry, etc.
- Reference Australian market-specific platforms: Trade Me (NZ but relevant), seek.com.au for recruitment ads, industry directories
- Cross-link to Cluster 01 (SEO) frequently — SEO and paid marketing are complementary and readers will move between these clusters
- Each supporting page: 1,500–2,500 words; pillar: 4,000+ words; blog posts: 800–1,500 words
