## GEO Notes & Considerations

- **Rapidly evolving landscape:** GEO is the newest of the three search disciplines covered by SecurityBlogs. Content should include publication dates and "last verified" notices given how quickly AI search features change.
- **Australian-specific context:** Google's AI Overviews roll-out pace in Australia may differ from the US. Track Australian SERP features specifically rather than relying solely on US-based GEO research.
- **Security industry authority signals:** Generative AI engines appear to favour highly authoritative, licence-verified, review-rich security businesses in local results. ASIAL membership and state security licence display are likely positive GEO signals.
- **Measurement gap:** GEO performance is harder to measure than SEO or AEO. Educate security company clients on proxy metrics (brand search volume, direct traffic, impression share) until better GEO analytics tools become available.
- **Zero-click risk:** AI Overviews and local AI results may reduce click-through rates even when your security company is featured. Frame GEO success around brand recall and awareness value alongside lead generation metrics.
- **All content in Australian English:** Use "optimise", "recognise", "organisation", "licence" (noun) throughout.
