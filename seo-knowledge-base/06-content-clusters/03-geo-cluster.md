- GEO is an emerging discipline — position SecurityBlogs Australia as an authoritative voice by publishing ahead of the curve
- All content in Australian English (optimise, recognise, colour, licence as noun)
- Distinguish between Google AI Overviews (Search Generative Experience / SGE successor) and other AI search tools
- Reference Australian search behaviour and local examples (Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, Perth)
- Include practical, actionable checklists — AI search changes fast and readers want guidance they can implement immediately
- Each supporting page: 1,500–2,500 words; pillar page: 4,000+ words; blog posts: 800–1,500 words
- Always link back to pillar and cross-link to Cluster 04 (AI Visibility) where relevant
