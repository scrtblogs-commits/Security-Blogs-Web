## SEO Notes & Considerations

- **Industry-specific trust signals:** Security companies face elevated scrutiny from Google's quality raters. Every page must include author credentials, industry certifications (ASIAL membership, security licence numbers where appropriate) and verifiable evidence of real expertise.
- **YMYL considerations:** Security advice falls under Google's "Your Money or Your Life" category. Content must demonstrate strong E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness).
- **Competitor landscape:** Major competitors in Australian security SEO content are generic digital marketing agencies with no security industry knowledge. SecurityBlogs' competitive advantage is deep vertical expertise.
- **Keyword difficulty:** "Security company SEO" head terms are moderately competitive. Long-tail and geo-modified terms (e.g., "security company SEO Sydney") offer faster ranking timelines of 3–6 months versus 6–12 months for broad terms.
- **Regulatory awareness:** Content about security companies should acknowledge Australian licensing requirements (state-based security licences) where relevant, as this builds topical authority and trust.
