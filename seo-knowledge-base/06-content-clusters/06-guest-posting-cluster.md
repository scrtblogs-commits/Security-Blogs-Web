## Notes for Writers

- This cluster has direct commercial intent — the "Guest Posting on SecurityBlogs Australia" supporting page is a service page, not just informational
- Australian English throughout
- Be transparent about what sponsored/editorial content means; ACCC guidelines require disclosure of paid placements
- Emphasise quality and relevance over volume — the security industry is a trust-dependent sector
- Reference ASIAL (Australian Security Industry Association Limited) publications and similar bodies as examples of reputable placement targets
- Do not promise specific ranking outcomes from link building — focus on authority, trust and traffic
- Each supporting page: 1,000–2,000 words; pillar page: 3,500+ words; blog posts: 700–1,200 words
