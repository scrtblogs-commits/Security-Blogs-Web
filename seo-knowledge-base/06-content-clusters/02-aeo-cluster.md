## Notes for Writers

- Always write answers in the first 40–60 words of any section (Google pulls snippet text from early in the content)
- Use question-led H2 and H3 headings throughout (e.g. "What is AEO for security companies?")
- Include a definition box or summary box at the top of each supporting page — these often become featured snippets
- Australian English throughout (optimise, organise, recognise, licence vs license, etc.)
- Reference real Australian voice search behaviours and local search intent (e.g. "security company near me Melbourne")
- Every FAQ should have a concise direct answer of 40–60 words followed by elaboration
