# SEO Ranking Competitors — Websites Competing for SecurityBlogs' Target Keywords

> **Scope:** Websites that rank for the same keywords SecurityBlogs Australia targets in Google Search. These may not be business competitors but they compete for the same search visibility — winning these positions drives traffic, authority, and AI citation potential.

**Last Updated:** June 2026
**Review Cycle:** Monthly (for keyword position tracking); Quarterly (for full document review)

---

## Overview

SEO ranking competitors are distinct from business competitors. A website that ranks #1 for "security company Melbourne" may not offer any of the same services as SecurityBlogs — it could be a Wikipedia article, a Reddit thread, or a comparison site. Understanding who holds the rankings SecurityBlogs needs is essential for:

1. **Identifying link building targets** — sites ranking above SecurityBlogs are candidates for content partnerships, guest posts, or surpassing with better content
2. **Understanding content gaps** — what format, depth, and angle does the ranking content use that SecurityBlogs should replicate or improve
3. **Building keyword strategies** — which domains dominate which keyword clusters, and where SecurityBlogs has the easiest path to ranking
4. **AI citation analysis** — high-ranking pages are frequently cited by AI engines; outranking them improves AI citation probability
